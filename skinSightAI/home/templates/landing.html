How Skin Problems Risking Your Life

Many skin diseases, such as eczema, psoriasis, and shingles, can cause intense itching, burning, pain, and discomfort. This can disrupt daily activities, sleep, and overall quality of life, impacting both physical and mental well-being. Damaged skin from conditions like cuts, blisters, or open sores can create pathways for bacteria, viruses, and fungi to enter the body. This increases the risk of secondary infections, which can be more severe and harder to treat than the original skin issue.

SkinSight Ai is not intended to perform diagnosis, but rather to provide users the ability to image, track, and better understand their moles.
